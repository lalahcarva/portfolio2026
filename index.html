About / Creative Identity

Hello! My name is Larissa Carvalho. I am a Graphic Designer and Illustrator based in Toronto. I enjoy turning ideas into simple, clear, and thoughtful visuals, and I’ve been interested in creativity for as long as I can remember, from drawing and painting to exploring ideas through games.

I truly care about doing good work and paying attention to details, whether I’m working on branding, social media, print, or digital design. I have experience in both freelance and in-house roles, creating content for different clients and managing projects from start to finish.

I am passionate about creativity and design. Outside of work, I stay inspired by art, photography, nature, and everyday moments, and I try to bring that curiosity into everything I create.
