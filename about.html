Hello! My name is Larissa Carvalho.

I am a Graphic Designer and Illustrator.

I’m a Graphic Designer based in Toronto, ON. I really enjoy creating and working with visual ideas, and for me design is something I naturally notice in everyday life. I like turning ideas into simple, clear, and thoughtful visuals. I’m always curious and I enjoy learning new tools and improving my work with each project.

Why Design?

Ever since I laid my hands on my first crayon, I knew I was destined for a life filled with colors, shapes, and endless creativity. I’ve liked drawing and creating for as long as I can remember. Even when I was younger, I enjoyed making things, whether it was drawing, painting, or imagining ideas through games. Over time, that turned into a real interest in design and how visuals can communicate ideas in a simple way. I like that design can tell a story without needing many words and for me that's something truly magical.

What Sets Me Apart?

What matters most to me is the process of turning ideas into clear, thoughtful visual solutions. I enjoy working with typography, layout, and color, and I’m especially interested in how design can feel both functional and expressive at the same time. I like to approach each project with care, whether it’s a brand piece, a social campaign, or a print design, focusing on detail, clarity, and intention. One of my biggest strengths is how much I care about the work I do, which means I will always give my best, I pay attention to the small details, and I take pride in delivering work that feels thoughtful and well executed. I pour my heart and soul into every project.

My Journey So Far

My experience spans both freelance and in-house design work. I’ve created visual identities, social media content, print materials, and digital campaigns for different clients and businesses. I also ran my own creative shop, where I designed and sold illustrated products, which gave me hands-on experience not only in design but also in communication, client management, and marketing. In my professional roles, I’ve worked on fast-paced projects that required organization, adaptability, and attention to detail. These experiences helped me grow not only as a designer but also as someone who understands how design fits into broader business and communication goals.

Beyond Design

Outside of work, I like to stay inspired by art, photography, nature, and discovering new places. I also enjoy slow walks, reading when it rains, listening to music, having coffee, watching TV shows, and playing games. I try to enjoy the little things in everyday life. I believe good design comes from staying curious and observing the world around you, and I try to bring that mindset into everything I create.

Let's Create Together

I’m always looking for opportunities to grow, learn, and contribute to projects that feel meaningful. I enjoy working with others, sharing ideas, and turning concepts into effective visuals. I am aways excited for new opportunities and I forward to connect with you.
